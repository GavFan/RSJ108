How to Write an Opinion Piece ...

Assembly time: 20 minutes
Awesome rating: 10% awesome

Are you wondering how to write an opinion piece that supports your ideas. It's really fun to make. Why don't you try to make it yourself? Follow the steps and you'll make a great written piece!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Paper and Pencil
  • Laptop
  • Sticky Notes
  • Your Brain!

  1. Get your sticky notes and pencil.

    Start writing down your thoughts about your specific topic(s) on each one.

  2. Orginize them to choose which one you would like to write about.

    Remember, you can change ideas or supporting evidence at anytime!

  3. Once you have chosen a topic, grab your paper and keep your pencil with you.

    Write on the paper the topic and each supporting eveidence as a bullet point.

    Under each bullet point, write about each supporting evidence with your ideas.

  4. Now, number your bullet points from strongest to weakest.

    Use your laptop to open a document software and start typing.

  5. 1. Introduction 2. Strongest Point 3. Body Paragraph 4. Body Paragraph 5. Weakest Point 6. Conclusion

    Reread and fix grammer and spelling.
